Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 6, 2025

Introduction

Quote English Daily ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide you with the best experience:

  • App Preferences: Your favorite quotes, language settings, and notification preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • Analytics: We may collect anonymous usage data to improve the app experience.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Provide and maintain our service
  • Save your favorite quotes and preferences
  • Send daily quote notifications (if enabled)
  • Improve and optimize our app

Data Storage

All your personal data, including favorites and settings, is stored locally on your device. We do not store any personal information on external servers.

Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services that collect information:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for more information.
  • Translation API: For translating quotes to your preferred language.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
